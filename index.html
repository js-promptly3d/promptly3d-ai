Transform Ideas Into Reality

Meet the world's first pure AI-powered 3D design platform that creates engineering-ready parts from natural language. No CAD software, no complex tools - just describe what you need and our AI generates professional-grade 3D models instantly.

How It Works

Describe Your Part

Just describe what you want to create in plain English - no CAD skills needed. "Make a bracket to hold my phone at 45 degrees" or "design a rounded box 10x5x2cm." Upload sketches, speak, or type naturally.

AI Processing

Unlike AI-assisted CAD tools, Promptly3D eliminates CAD software entirely. Our language models directly create precise 3D geometry and apply engineering constraints from your descriptions.

Preview & Refine

Refine your design through natural conversation. Say "add a 5mm fillet to all edges" or "make it 20% stronger" - our AI understands design intent and updates the 3D model instantly.

Get Manufacturing Files

Get complete manufacturing-ready files instantly: STEP for machining, optimized STL for 3D printing, and professional technical drawings with tolerances.

Order Your Part

Choose materials and finishes in plain English, then connect instantly with verified manufacturers. Compare quotes and track your part from production to delivery.
